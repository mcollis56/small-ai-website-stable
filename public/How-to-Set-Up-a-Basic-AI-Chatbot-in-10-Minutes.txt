# How to Set Up a Basic AI Chatbot in 10 Minutes

A comprehensive guide for small businesses to implement AI chatbots quickly and efficiently.

## Quick Setup Guide
1. Choose your platform (ChatGPT API, Dialogflow, etc.)
2. Define your chatbot's purpose and responses
3. Set up basic conversation flows
4. Test and refine
5. Deploy to your website

This resource provides step-by-step instructions for implementation.
